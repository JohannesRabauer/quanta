PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The SV-2400 represents VertiCrop's flagship residential/commercial 
vertical farming system. Designed for year-round production of 
leafy greens, herbs, and compact fruiting vegetables in climate-
controlled indoor environments.

HYDROPONIC SYSTEM

Type: Nutrient Film Technique (NFT) with recirculation

Water Reservoir:
  Capacity:         45 liters (11.9 gallons)
  Material:         Food-grade polyethylene
  Fill interval:    Weekly (avg)
  pH monitoring:    Automatic sensors

Pump Specifications:
  Flow rate:        2.5 L/min per tier
  Power:            45W submersible pump
  Duty cycle:       15 min on / 15 min off (adjustable)
  Noise level:      <35 dB

Nutrient Delivery:
  Distribution:     Drip manifolds per tier
  Mixing:           Automated dosing system
  EC control:       1.2-2.4 mS/cm range
  pH control:       5.5-6.5 automatic adjustment

Water Recovery:
  Recycling rate:   97.3%
  Daily loss:       ~1.2 L (evaporation + plant uptake)
  Filter system:    Dual-stage (mechanical + carbon)
