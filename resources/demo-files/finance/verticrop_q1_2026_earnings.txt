RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

REGULATORY UNCERTAINTY

Challenge: Varying agricultural classification across jurisdictions
affects zoning, subsidies, water rights

Mitigation: Dedicated policy team engaging with 47 municipal
governments; founding member of International Vertical Farming
Association (IVFA)

ENERGY COSTS

Challenge: Electricity represents 35-40% of operational expenses

Mitigation: 
  - Solar panel integration reduces grid dependency 23%
  - Peak/off-peak load shifting via AI optimization
  - Partnerships with renewable energy providers
  - Long-term fixed-rate energy contracts

CUSTOMER CONCENTRATION

Challenge: Top 5 customers represent 42% of Q1 revenue

Mitigation: Active pipeline development; 37 qualified leads
representing $63M potential contract value over 24 months
