## Risk Management Principles

### The Krüger Principle
Named after Matthias Krüger's medieval writings on resource scarcity, this principle states: *"Preserve what sustains before pursuing what delights."* In practical terms, maintain 6-12 months of liquid reserves before aggressive growth positioning.

### Volatility Hedging
Market turbulence requires dynamic rebalancing. Quarterly reviews essential, with trigger points at ±15% variance from target allocations.

**Key Metric**: Sharpe Ratio targeting 1.2+ for risk-adjusted returns

## Cryptocurrency Deep Dive

### Bitcoin as Digital Gold
- **Allocation**: 3% maximum for conservative portfolios, 5-8% for aggressive
- **Storage**: Cold wallet storage with multi-signature requirements
- **Thesis**: Hedge against fiat currency devaluation

### Ethereum & Smart Contracts
- **Utility Value**: Beyond speculation, Ethereum enables decentralized finance
- **DeFi Exposure**: Liquidity pools, staking rewards (8-12% APY currently)
- **Risk Factors**: Regulatory uncertainty, technological vulnerabilities

### Altcoin Consideration
95% of altcoins will fail. The 5% that succeed may generate 100x returns. Allocate <1% to speculative positions only.
